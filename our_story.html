The Edge

Do you ever look at something and think: OK, but what is the point?
Aesthetically it could be pretty, but then what? Purpose. Storytelling.
Narrative. An arc. Be it film, a static image, a brand. What is your purpose?

About Us

Burgers & Hoodies is a creative agency, based in Dubai established in 2020.
We are an independent agency with a passion to create bold and well-
crafted films; blurring the lines between cinema, art and commerce, all
through the means of storytelling. Our purpose is to challenge the way
people see things no matter the size of the project. We want to create
a buzz.

The Vision

One of the most things we heard growing up in our region, is that the
standard of work is not at a global level; or that there is a lack of
artistry in what is being produced. To that we say; Keep your eyes open,
along side a lot of emerging talented people, we at Burgers & Hoodies
would like to be part of the path being paved for game changing
standards.
